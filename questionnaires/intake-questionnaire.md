# Intake Questionnaire

This questionnaire is used to capture the minimum information needed to register and review a proposed AI use case in the **AI Use-Case Governance Registry** repository.

It is designed to support:
- use case registration
- impact assessment
- preliminary governance classification
- control review
- governance decision-making

The questionnaire supports a repository-level governance process. It does **not** determine legal classification, conformity assessment, certification, or compliance.

## Instructions

Complete this questionnaire before starting governance review.

Where information is not yet known, state that clearly rather than guessing.

The questionnaire is intentionally simple. It is designed to collect enough information for preliminary governance review without turning intake into a heavy process.

## Notes on use

This questionnaire is an intake tool for repository-level governance.

It supports:
- consistent early information gathering
- traceable governance review
- clearer handoff into the registry and assessment artifacts

It does **not**:
- perform formal legal analysis
- determine obligations under the EU AI Act
- replace privacy, security, legal, compliance, or risk review
- establish conformity with ISO/IEC 42001
- implement NIST AI RMF as a checklist
